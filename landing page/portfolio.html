RFID based Attendence using Nodemcu & google sheet

The RFID-based attendance system using Google Sheets combines the power of Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) technology with the convenience of cloud-based data management through Google Sheets. Each participant or student is assigned a unique RFID tag, which is read by an RFID reader upon entry. As individuals present their RFID tags to the reader, the system captures their unique identifiers and automatically logs the attendance data to a Google Sheets spreadsheet in real-time. This process eliminates the need for manual attendance tracking, reducing errors and saving valuable time.
